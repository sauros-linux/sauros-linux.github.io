about saurOS

what even is saurOS?

saurOS was a project I did for a class in college. Effectively, it was (and still is) just a modified archiso with some nice little bits and bobs.

why bulbasaur?

I like him.

can i do X with this?

Do whatever you want except for sell it. As a note, I do not own any of the pieces of artwork in the distribution nor do I own the copyright of Bulbasaur. I may wish I did, but that is not the world we live in. Keep that in mind if you want to do anything with this project.

why maintain this if it was a college project?

Well, for one it's funny. I'm not taking this seriously, and if anyone actually does then I'll be shocked. If I do update this, the extent would just be adding more Bulbasaur art and updating the mirrors. I likely won't be accepting pull requests, this is just my stupid little project and I don't intend on ever taking this seriously.
